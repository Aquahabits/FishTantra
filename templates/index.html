About FishTantra

FishTantra is an intelligent fisheries-focused platform designed to identify fish species from images using deep learning. It aims to assist students, researchers, aquaculturists, and fisheries professionals with fast and reliable species recognition.

  • Deep learning–based image classification
  • Support for freshwater & marine species
  • Taxonomy & aquaculture integration (upcoming)
  • Research & education oriented
